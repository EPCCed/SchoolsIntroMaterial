Definitions

  • Oxford Dictionary: An electronic device which is capable of receiving information (data) in a particular form and of performing a sequence of operations in accordance with a predetermined but variable set of procedural instructions (program) to produce a result in the form of information or signals.
  • Free Online Dictionary of Computing: A machine that can be programmed to manipulate symbols. Computers can perform complex and repetitive procedures quickly, precisely and reliably and can quickly store and retrieve large amounts of data.
These are just for references - the aim is to give idea that even after decades of computer use, an easy definition is still hard but students should attempt after the lessons to define it themselves.

Function

Computers can be grouped by their intended function.

  • Server
  • Workstation
  • Personal Computing Devices
  • Embedded
Servers are something anyone who has used the internet have accessed - commonly people will have heard of the term web server, which is a server designed to handle and process web pages. Other servers could be games server (xbox live, playstation equivalent), bank servers, email servers. Workstations were often what people used for high end or data processing tasks - term has fallen out of use a little now. PCD - this is all the computers people use, laptops, tablets, etc. Embedded - this is where a computer is used in a "hidden form" - washing machines, cars, planes. It is a computer designed to deal with a specific task sets and do it saefly and quickly.

What does it mean for us?

Computers are now a part of daily life.

Modern science, engineering and medicine are dependent on computers for progress and operation.

Modern Communications rely on computers in many forms.

Increased availability of news and culture around the world.

Important that we understand the capabilities and limitations of computers

Modern society has become intertwined with technology for better or worse. This impact is important to reliase and the students could think of other ways that it has impacted them.
It is important to place emphasis that even with all the talk and advances in AI and machine learning that people still direct things and are the driving force. People can learn and make leaps computers cannot.

Important

Computers are tools.

People have to use them sensibly.

Don't use them for tasks they are not suitable for.
